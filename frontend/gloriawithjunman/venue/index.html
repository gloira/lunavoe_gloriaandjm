Venue & Stay

Stone Villas Uluwatu · A clifftop villa overlooking the Indian Ocean.

Wedding Venue
Stone Villas Uluwatu is a private clifftop villa in Uluwatu, with views of the ocean, tropical gardens, and a lawn where our ceremony and dinner will take place.

The exact address and arrival details will be shared in our guest group closer to the date, along with transportation arrangements for those not staying on site.
Where to Stay
Some guests will stay inside the main villa complex, and others will stay at nearby hotels or villas in Uluwatu. We'll coordinate rooms and share confirmed options in our guest chat group once bookings are finalized.

If you're planning to extend your stay or book your own place, we recommend staying within the Uluwatu area so it's easy to get to the venue.
