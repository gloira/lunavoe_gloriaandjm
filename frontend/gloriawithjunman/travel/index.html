Travel Guide

Ideas for slow mornings, sunset spots, and post‑wedding adventures.

Around Uluwatu · Before the Wedding

Friday and the wedding morning are free time — here are some ideas if you'd like to explore nearby:

  • Cafes with views in Uluwatu for brunch and coffee.
  • Beach clubs and sunset bars along the cliff for golden hour.
  • Relaxed beaches where you can swim, read, or just do nothing.
After the Wedding · Keep Exploring

If you're staying longer in Bali, some popular areas to spend a few more days:

  • Seminyak & Canggu – cafes, shopping, and sunsets.
  • Jimbaran – seafood by the beach and calm evenings.
  • Ubud – rice terraces, temples, and spa days.
